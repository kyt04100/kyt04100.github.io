A software-defined radio (SDR) testbed for over-the-air validation of fluid antenna system channel models, operating at 2.5 GHz with LTE-like OFDM signaling. The testbed enables direct comparison between measured spatial correlation and analytical models (e.g., Bessel-function correlation).
