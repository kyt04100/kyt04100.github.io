Tri-Hybrid MIMO extends conventional hybrid beamforming with a third, antenna-domain (electromagnetic) processing layer — e.g., reconfigurable pixel antennas — enabling scalable spatial degrees of freedom without proportional RF-chain growth.

I work on this architecture in the context of **AI-native 6G MIMO for physical AI and robotics**, where the uplink multimodal sensor payload is the key wireless bottleneck.
