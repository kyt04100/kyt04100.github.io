Fluid Antenna Systems (FAS) reconfigure the effective antenna position ("port") to exploit spatial diversity within a compact aperture. My work focuses on **pixel-based reconfigurable antenna (PRA)** implementations of FAS, including:

- Optimal port selection under sparse channel conditions
- Channel estimation (LMMSE / learning-based) with limited port observations
- The **reality gap** between theoretical spatial covariance models and measured covariance from a 2.5 GHz SDR testbed with LTE-like OFDM signaling
